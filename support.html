ABOUT ME

I'm a solo developer and a lifelong anime fan. Shadow Gym began with one idea: what if leveling up in real life felt like leveling up in Solo Leveling? So I built all of it myself — the quests, the ranks, the Arena, the Forge, the art and the code.

This isn't a weekend hobby — I'm doing it for a living. No team, no studio, no ads, no paywalls. Just one Hunter shipping updates one commit at a time.
