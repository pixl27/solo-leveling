PUSH-UPS
CHEST • SHOULDERS • TRICEPS
HOW TO PERFORM
  1. Start in a high plank position with hands shoulder-width apart
  2. Keep your body in a straight line from head to heels
  3. Lower your chest to the ground by bending your elbows
  4. Push back up to the starting position
  5. Repeat for the desired number of repetitions
VARIATIONS
Wide Push-ups
Diamond Push-ups
Decline Push-ups
Incline Push-ups
Archer Push-ups
One-Arm Push-ups
TRAINING TIPS
  • Focus on controlled movements rather than speed
  • Breathe out when pushing up, breathe in when lowering
  • Keep your core engaged throughout the movement
  • Rest 60-90 seconds between sets for strength gains
